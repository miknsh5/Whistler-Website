The Whistler Trip 2013: Old School

March 7-10, 2013 - Whistler B.C.



Let's go ahead and assume you didn't think we really had our acts together this year. After all, we're pretty old. We have responsibilities now. We've had some good years, and maybe it's just time to put this old tradition to rest. Call it good. Move on to the rest of our lives.


Well shut up and find some neon leather pants. This is happening.

This thing's actually coming together!

Well it takes a village and so far, our village is delivering. Volunteers have heeded the call, and things are starting to take shape. Here's the business:

The Pre Party - As mentioned on Monday, we've got a Whistler "practice" party lined up for this weekend at the Smith's. Feel free to use this as an opportunity to test out your costume, brush up on your slap cup and crash between ski days this weekend.

Food and Drink! - Bills is taking care of the bulk alcohol purchase. Do you have a favorite item you want to make sure is on the list? Email Bills before this weekend. Also, while the menu is TBD, do you have a favorite snack you want Jeff to grab? Let him know. Otherwise, general breakfast and snack fare will be selected at random.

Drivers - We need them! Email Ben.

Lastly, I give you the third and final 2009 Whistler video to the right. Enjoy.
