Clayton's Corner

I've never killed anyone.

You know what the placeholder text said before I typed this? It said, "This is a terrible idea." You know why that's fucking retarded? Because I've never killed anyone. There was that one time with Bills and the Jeep. And that time I rolled the Jeep with Becky was a fairly close call - but that's the point - it wouldn't seem so reeeeeal if it wasn't a close call. I'm getting distracted.

It's getting deep.

What really matters is that deep down in your heart, there's a little piece that wants to get a little wild sometimes. Maybe set something on fire. Run from the cops a bit. What I'm telling you is, it's O.K. Everyone feels this way. Mike Bills is the perfect example - you get excited just a little bit... and next thing you know you're going 93mph in a Peterbilt semi with a stolen load of cigarettes somewhere in South Dakota. Some people need a little helping along. I'm the guy that's there for you when you need it most. If you need pushed over the edge - whether it's a little kicker or a rocky cliff with no landing - I'm there to give you that shove.

Now... I've been doing a little shopping.

I've got that motherly feeling about the whistler party and just want it to be perfect for you and your little friends - so I made you a short film. Yep - a little film with just the tip of what's to come - and I just put it in there for a second. After taking a peek into my mind I think you'll realize we're really pretty similar human beings. We probably have a lot of the same thoughts, goals, and life principals.
