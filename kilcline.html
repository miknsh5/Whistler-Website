A Personal Message from Beth Kilcline

THIS WHISTLER....steel your mind, open your heart, and ready your liver, because the Kilcline twins are turning 30! 

Let's face it - We're getting older. Sure, in past Whistlers, we shredded gnar.  But then we came home and chugged beer underwater in hot tubs, climbed f'in mountains, and broke doors/plates/stools/Nash.  Now, some people say we are too "mature" for these apres ski shenanigans.  

We beg to differ.  

This Whistler weekend, the Kilcline sisters will not go quietly into this good night. Instead, we will rage, rage... and get kicked out of our condos for noise violations!!

So here is our message to you: Ski your heart out during the day, but be ready for an all-out costume party extravaganza on Friday night and dancing in the Village on Saturday!
