With great power comes great responsibility.

"Play it cool... don't sound desperate... Oh god, please help!!!"

Head Chefs - 2 Positions

Description: Operate a multi-hundred dollar budget to execute a hearty meal of your choosing for roughly 25 people. Candidates are able to work within the circumstances presented (condo kitchen), but can also creatively use their surroundings (snow?).

These positions are also responsibile for purchasing (using group funds) standard breakfast fare.

Reward: You get to choose what we eat! Also, everyone will love you. You can literally do no wrong for the rest of the trip.

VP of Spirits and Ales - 1 Position

Description: Coordinate alcohol aqusition, transportation and distribution. Will likely leverage duty free. Manage alcohol slush fund. Walk fine like between game beer and delicious after ski cocktails. Actually, just get both.

Reward: While the chefs assume their the most loved, this person actually is. Could probably slant the alcohol selection to your favorites. 

Transportation Consultant - 1 Position

Description: Basically make sure everyone gets there. Coordinate drivers. Work closely with the VP of Spirits/Ales.

Reward: You have no responsibilities once everyone gets to Whistler.

Senior Executive Party Planner - 2 Positions

Description: Perhaps the most critical role. Plan and execute one (1) house party and one (1) night of going out in Whistler. Added challenge: It's Beth's birthday and she loves to dance.

Reward: Kilcline love, control over party theme

Art and Music Director - 1 Position

Description: Design a weekend long playlist via the method of you choosing to sustain at least 2 significant parties, large amounts of general milling about, a birthday, ski porn viewing, slap pong and regretful mornings.

Reward: You're the new Padberg.
